About Me

My name is Cristian Contreras, I am a So Cal native born and raised, and I am at an early point in what I hope will be a long journey in the accounting field. I graduated from High School in 2006 and attended California State University Los Angeles from 2006-2011, where I earned my Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting. After graduation, I was fortunate enough to land a job for a wholesale horse feed distributor in El Monte, CA called Angel’s Feeds, Inc. Over the last 3+ years, I’ve learned so much about my profession and continue to do so on a daily basis. After an amazing 3+ years at Angel’s Feeds Inc., I feel that I am at a point where I am ready to take the next step in my career. I am ready to take on new challenges, which I feel fully prepared for given all my preparation up until this point and I am very excited to find out where my next adventure will take me!
